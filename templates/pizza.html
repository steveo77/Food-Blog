-Via 313: was started by two brothers from Detroit that wanted to bring authentic Detroit pizza two Austin. They have two locations, one is on Rainey street attached to Craft Pride. This pizza has thick chewy crust with the outside lined with different blends of cheese, then they put on a thick layer of cheese on top and add toppings, top that with a thick layer of sauce.

-Home Slice: Home Slice Pizza was created by Jen and Joseph Strickland and there partner Terri Hannifin back in 2004. It is a New York style pizza, large slices with a crispy crust, generous amounts of cheese and fresh toppings. It is located on South Congress in the trendy South Austin area. They have two locations right next to each other, one for dine in and one for take out. Because of its popularity I would suggest calling ahead.
